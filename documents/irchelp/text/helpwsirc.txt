To obtain WSIRC help, use
/msg ai- <topic>
where <topic> is two words from the list below.
Like: /msg ai- wsirc dcc or /msg ai- wsirc registration
.
WSIRC Compare.......WSIRC DCC
WSIRC Expire........WSIRC Finger
WSIRC Manual........WSIRC Modes
WSIRC MultiSessions.WSIRC Registration
WSIRC Scripts.......WSIRC Scrolling
WSIRC Sources.......WSIRC Status
WSIRC Versions......WSIRC Chameleon
WSIRC PCTCP.........WSIRC Trumpet
.
